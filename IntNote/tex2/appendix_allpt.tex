
Figures~\ref{fig:Fits4GeV}--\ref{fig:Fits8_10GeV} show the fits to the 
  \Dphi\ distributions for several \ptbar\ ranges.
It is seen that the plots for the 4--5~\GeV\ \ptbar\ range, shown in Figure~\ref{fig:Fits4_5GeV},
  are completely dominated bys statistical fluctuations.
Figures~\ref{fig:WidthsAllPt} and \ref{fig:CSAllPt} show the widths and \TAA\ scaled yields of the $Y^{\mathrm{corr}}(\Dphi)$ 
  distributions (Eq.~\ref{eq:fit_func}--\ref{eq:signal_func}).
These plots also demonstrate that the results for the 4--5~\GeV\ \ptbar\ interval
  are dominated by statistical uncertainties.
This the reason why the nominal results in this analysis are made for the \ptbar>5~\GeV\ interval.

Note that the systematic uncertainties for the new \ptbar\ bins
  shown in these plots have not been checked thoroughly,
  but instead mechanically evaluated by running the same 
  variations as those performed on the nominal analysis.
While this give a good first estimate of the systematic uncertainty,
  it is not final. 
These results are not intended for the paper but only for the internal note.
