This section describes the evaluatiuon of the systematic 
  uncertainties for the \pp\ measurements.
One change from the sourced of the \PbPb\ uncertainties is the following:
  the uncertainty related to inclusion of a third order harmonic in
  Eq.~\ref{eq:fit_func} is dropped.
It is replaced by an uncertainty that instead includes the variation 
  in the results when completely excluding 
	the $v_{2,2}$ term in~Eq.\eqref{eq:fit_func}.
This is because the underlying event contribution in the \pp\
  measurements is quite small compared to that in the \PbPb\ events:
  compare Figure~\ref{fig:fit_PbPb_tight} and Figure~\ref{fig:fit_pp},
	(note that Figure~\ref{fig:fit_PbPb_tight} is zero-suppressed).
At the same time, from prior measurements, the \vn\ in \pp\
  collisions are known to be significantly smaller than those
  in \PbPb\ collisions.
Additionally in the \pp\ \vn\ measurements a subtraction
  is always needed to correct for non-flow effects,
	which is not possible in the present measurements.
Given these conditions, it is more likely that the $v_{2,2}$ term
  in the \pp\ measurement is biased from remnants of the near-side 
	jet peak rather than a true UE modulation.
For this reason this change in the source of sytematic uncertainty
  for the \pp\ measurements is made.



The other sources of uncertainties are the same as that for the 
  \PbPb\ and are not described here again.
\iftoggle{IncludeYields}
{
  The luminosity uncertainty for the \pp\ data is 1.6\%.
  Trigger eff uncertainties and SF uncertainties for 
    the trigger are $\sim1$\% and included as overall uncertainties
    on the \pp\ C.S.
}{}
The uncertainties arising from
\begin{enumerate}
\item Choice of muon selection: Tight (default) vs Medium.
\item Effect of uncertainties on reconstruction efficiencies.
\item Dependence on the \Deta\ cut.
\item Dependence on the Fit model (not included for the $\Gamma$).
\item Excluding the Fourier term in~Eq.\eqref{eq:fit_func}.
\item Alternate efficiency for correcting for the \Mmumu\ cuts.
\item Alternate PDFs for correcting for the Drell-Yan pairs.
\end{enumerate}
  are shown in the remaining plots in this section.
The uncertainties are summarized in Tables~\ref{tab:systematics_pp}
  and~\ref{tab:systematics2_pp} for the widths and for $\Gamma$, respectively.
