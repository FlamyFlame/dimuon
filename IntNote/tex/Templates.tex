In order to ascertain our signal fraction we fit our data in $\Delta p/p$ pair significance.
HIJING Starlight MC samples from a non-UPC (ultra-peripheral collision) dimuon analysis for signal, and Jet data-overlay MC samples JZ0-JZ5 with 2018 conditions for background are used.
$\Delta p/p$ significance distributions were made for each of these from single muons, an example of which is shown in the left-hand side of Figure~\ref{fig:Dpopsigdist}.

The single muon $\Delta p/p$ significance distributions were then convoluted and resampled to make signal-signal, signal-background, and background-background distributions as shown in the right-hand side of Figure~\ref{fig:Dpopsigdist}.


Also to be noted, as the $p_T>4$ cut was based in $p_{T1}$ and $p_{T2}$ was integrated, a distribution of $p_{T2}$ was stored and randomly sampled to choose which $p_{T2}$ $\Delta p/p$ significance distribution to be used to create the pair significance.
