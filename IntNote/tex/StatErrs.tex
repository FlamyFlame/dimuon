
The statistical uncertainties for the width of the $Y^{\mathrm{corr}} (\Dphi)$ term in Eq.~\eqref{eq:signal_func}
  are estimated using toys, where $dN/d\Dphi$ distribution are resampled and refitted using Eq.~\eqref{eq:fit_func},
  and the standard deviations in the yields and RMS-widths in the resamplings are taken as 
  the corresponding uncertainties. 
Figures~\ref{fig:stat_err1}--\ref{fig:stat_err2} show the distribution of the widths in for the toys,
  for the centrality intervals used in this analysis.
